1. **Disconnection ya Game Quit Rule:** Agar koi player disconnect ho jata hai ya unki game kisi wajah se left ho jati hai, toh unhe automatically 4th position mil jayegi. Match kisi bhi surat mein dobara nahi shuru hoga. 2. **Points Distribution:** - 1st place: 20 points - 2nd place: 15 points - 3rd place: 10 points - 4th place: 5 points Total 4 matches honge. Saare points end mein accumulate honge, aur sirf wo team jiske paas sabse zyada points honge, wo next round ke liye qualify karegi. Baaki ki 3 teams eliminate ho jayengi. 3. **Tie-Breaker Rule:** Agar teams ke points tie ho jate hain, toh tie-breaker match hoga.
